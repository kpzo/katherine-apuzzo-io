About

I am a mother, photographer, and budding programmer. Currently I process film by hand for a living. I measure and mix chemicals, then pour them over film I have loaded in the dark. This creates, with practice, a proper exposure which can then be used for scanning or printing in the darkroom. Long story short, I bring film to life!
